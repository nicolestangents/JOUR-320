About Me

Nicole is a fourth-year student at Northwestern University studying journalism and legal studies, with a minor in political science. Having grown up in three politically divided cities — Hong Kong, Shanghai, and Taipei — she quickly developed an interest in consuming news media to contextualize the tensions she saw every day. Though Nicole hopes to attend law school, she is still passionate about reporting and hopes the skills picked up through journalism will benefit her legal journey. Explore some of her best work here, along with her experimentation with data visualizations.
